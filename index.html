About Haiti

Haiti is a Caribbean country located on the island of Hispaniola, which it shares with the Dominican Republic to the east. Despite the visible traces left by the 2010 earthquake, many points of interest from the 19th century are still intact, such as the La Ferrière Citadel, a fortress overlooking the island. The surrounding ruins of the Sans Souci palace, a former baroque-style royal residence of King Henry I, are also significant landmarks in the country's history.
