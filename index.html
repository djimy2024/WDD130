Haiti

Haiti is a Caribbean country located on the island of Hispaniola, a territory it shares with the Dominican Republic to the east. Although the 2010 earthquake left visible traces, many points of interest from the beginning of the 19th century are still intact, for example the La Ferrière citadel, a fortress overlooking the island, or the surrounding ruins of the Sans Souci palace. , former baroque-style royal residence of King Henry I.
